EtherTechna

A social journalism app focused on spreading technical skills and ideas in the arts, sciences, and humanities. Membership allows users to write and share there thoughts as well as issue claps to content they enjoy.

Built using Ruby on Rails and Postgres on the back end, with React + Redux on the front. Crafted in the likeness of Medium.com

Pintrigue

A social media app where the community shares suggestions and interesting finds from across the web. Users upload URLs and images using a clean drag and drop interface.

Implemented with the MERN (MongoDB, Express, React, Node) solution stack, utilizing React-Stack-Grid and React-Dropzone. Modeled after Pinterest.com

Team Partners: John-Michael Riley and Jasmine John.

  • Repo

Just Decide

A mobile-first app that assists groups of people come to concensus. A person asks a question to their friends or family (e.g. 'What movie should we see tonight?'), everyone votes, and a decision is made.

Utilizes the MERN solution stack with GraphQL for back-end requests and Apollo for memory caching.

A collaboration with Lisa H Steele and Richard W Robinson.

  • Repo

Pharmacist Zenny

A web game inspired by combining two important pillars in my life:
My Mother and Nintendo's Dr. Mario.

Players match 4 colors in a row or column to remove diseases from the bottle. Allow the bottle to overflow and game over.

Written with practical OOP principles in plain Vanilla Javascript and HTML5's Canvas. Vector graphics manually hard-coded in Canvas and raster graphics illustrated by hand.
